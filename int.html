Goals of our Stream

The research topic is "A Comparative Study between the Effectiveness of APEC's Modular Learning System and the Teacher-Centered Traditional Learning." The content will be informative and smooth, with interviews and music and video audio. The video will be an informative one, with no changes or breaks. The content will be streamed and a documentary, and the creators will ensure the videos are not copyrighted if used.

Summary of Stream

The stream starts off with an introduction to the main content which is the comparison of effectiveness between modular and traditional teaching and followed by the credits of members of the research. The stream then showcases and explains the “modular” learning approach. The stream proceeds to the first data gathering part of the stream where the researchers conduct surveys in different classrooms. After the survey part, a short “day in the life” segment took part to give viewers a deeper idea on what the researchers are trying to decipher. Interviews were held after that segment where the interviewees gave their thoughts and insights on the topic, including one special interview. After those interviews, conclusions were made and the video ends. The overall stream summarized researchers pursuing knowledge on their topic “ The comparison of effectiveness between modular and traditional teaching” and presenting their methodology and findings of the study through the documentary.
